LOREM   IPSUM

Launched in 2011, LOREM IPSUM is a multi-cuisine restaurant which presents the most scrumptious dishes and beverages. The café serves as the coffee shop for the resort in the day, and turns into the best restaurant in Lonavala for a spirited dining in the evening. The restaurant is eloquently designed which creates an aura of sophistication all around. The design itself is a beautiful blend of modernity and nostalgic charm.

LOREM IPSUM has become one of the most preferred tourist spots near Mumbai and Pune, and for multiple reasons. While the adventure park and resort have gained humongous popularity, other major reason is the food served at LOREM IPSUM, which is the best in the mountain valley of Lonavala. There are about five multi-cuisine restaurants which serve exquisite cuisines that will leave you absolutely delighted.

MENU

LOREM IPSUM serves some of the most delicious dishes belonging to various cuisines, right from Continental, to Indian and Oriental. The chefs behind the curtains are highly trained and experienced in their respective genres, and prepare the best delicacies which will ring your taste buds, and are also pleasing to the eye. Also, the staff is highly professional, and is extremely polite and helpful. Some of the most popular dishes served at LOREM IPSUM include Amritsari Grilled Fish, Pepper Crusted Burger, Chicken Tikka With Shallots & Charred Peppers, etc. The restaurant is open 24×7, and takes pride in breaking the myth that good cuisines stop at 11’oclock. LOREM IPSUM is, in fact, redefining the entire concept of late night dining, with eclectic mix of mouth-watering cuisines being served throughout the night.
